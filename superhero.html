Meet Our Superhero!
Supremo

Who is Supremo and what can he do?

Supremo is a living being superhero from mars who can see in the dark, he can monitor his bodily temprature so he is not affected by changes in temperature as he has a thick layer of skin, can move freely through space as he is not affected by any type of radiation, and is a boneless creature.

Supremo provides company to crew members that help them with their loneliness problems.

Supremo only sleeps one hour in a week so mostly he will be awake in case of any emergency

How can Supremo overcome the risks?

He acts as a buddy for crew members to help with their mental health.

He can tolerate changes in temperature.

He is a boneless creature which means he is not affected by bone loss or decompression sickness.

He stays awake most of the time therefore he always ready and he can withstand a long period of time in space.
